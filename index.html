❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to create an account to use these tools?

No account is needed at all. All 9 AI tools are available instantly without any registration, login, or signup. Just open the website and start using any tool immediately.

Is my data stored when I use these tools?

No. All processing happens entirely in your browser. We do not store, save, or share any text, proposals, notes, or other data you enter in our tools. Your information stays completely private on your device.

Are these tools completely free? Any hidden charges?

Yes, completely free — no hidden charges, no premium plan, no credit card required. All 9 tools are 100% free forever. We earn through the advertisements shown on the website, which keeps everything free for you.

Do these tools work on mobile phones?

Yes! All tools are fully responsive and work perfectly on mobile phones, tablets, and computers. The interface automatically adjusts to your screen size for the best experience on any device.

Can I use the AI-generated content commercially?

Yes, you can use the generated content for your business and commercial purposes. However, we recommend reviewing and personalizing all AI-generated content before using it professionally, as it is a template that needs your specific details added.

Why is the Proposal Auditor score low for my proposal?

The tool checks for four key elements: word count (500+ words), executive summary, budget information, and timeline/schedule. If any of these are missing from your proposal, the score will be lower. Add these missing elements and re-analyze to improve your score.

How do I save the QR code image?

After generating your QR code, right-click on the QR code image and select "Save image as" from the menu. Choose a location on your computer and save it. On mobile, press and hold the image and select "Download image" or "Save to gallery".

The tool is not working — what should I do?

First, refresh the page and try again. Make sure you have filled all required fields before clicking the button. If the problem continues, try clearing your browser cache or using a different browser like Chrome or Edge. You can also contact us at saeedfazli@gmail.com and we will help you quickly.

Can I use these tools without internet?

Most tools (Proposal Auditor, LinkedIn Ice Breaker, Payment Chaser, Case Study Builder, Meeting Minutes, Review Reply, PC Config Advisor, Image Compressor) work after the page loads even with slow internet. The QR Code Generator requires internet connection as it uses an external API to generate codes.

📚 How to Use — Complete Tutorial

Step-by-step guide for all 9 AI tools. Follow these simple steps to get the best results!

1️⃣ AI Proposal Auditor 📝

Purpose: Analyze your business proposal and get a score with improvement suggestions.

How to use step by step:

  1. Click on "AI Proposal Auditor" card on home page
  2. Copy your complete business proposal text
  3. Paste it in the large text box
  4. Click the "Analyze Proposal" button
  5. Wait 2-3 seconds for results
  6. You will see a score out of 100 with a progress bar
  7. Read each feedback point carefully
  8. Fix the missing items (executive summary, budget, timeline)
  9. Paste the improved proposal again and re-analyze

💡 Pro Tips:

  • Proposals with 500+ words score higher
  • Always include executive summary, budget and timeline
  • Target score: 75 or above for a winning proposal

2️⃣ LinkedIn Ice Breaker 💼

Purpose: Generate a professional personalized message to connect with someone on LinkedIn.

How to use step by step:

  1. Open LinkedIn and find the person you want to connect with
  2. Copy their profile URL from the browser address bar
  3. Come back to this tool and click "LinkedIn Ice Breaker"
  4. Paste the LinkedIn URL in the input field
  5. Click "Generate Message"
  6. Read the generated message
  7. Click generate again if you want a different message
  8. Personalize the message with the person's name and specific details
  9. Go back to LinkedIn and send the connection request with this message

💡 Pro Tips:

  • Always personalize — mention something specific from their profile
  • Keep the message under 300 characters for best results
  • Send connection requests Tuesday to Thursday for best response rates

3️⃣ Late Payment Chaser 💰

Purpose: Generate professional payment reminder emails for overdue invoices.

How to use step by step:

  1. Click "Late Payment Chaser" on home page
  2. Select the reminder type from dropdown:
    • Day 7 — First gentle reminder
    • Day 14 — Second follow-up
    • Day 21 — Urgent reminder
    • Day 30 — Final notice before legal action
  3. Click "Generate Email"
  4. Copy the subject line and email body separately
  5. Replace all placeholders: [Client Name], [Invoice Number], [Amount], [Due Date]
  6. Add your actual business name and contact information
  7. Send from your professional email account

💡 Pro Tips:

  • Always replace ALL placeholders before sending
  • Send emails Tuesday to Thursday 9am-11am for best open rates
  • Keep tone professional even for Day 30 final notice
  • Always have a signed contract before you start work

4️⃣ Case Study Builder 📊

Purpose: Turn client feedback and project details into a professional case study for your portfolio.

How to use step by step:

  1. Click "Case Study Builder" on home page
  2. Gather your project information:
    • Client feedback or testimonial
    • What problem you solved
    • What results you achieved
  3. Paste all this information in the text area
  4. Click "Generate Case Study"
  5. Review the generated case study structure
  6. Copy the result
  7. Paste in Word or Google Docs and customize with real numbers
  8. Replace the sample percentages with your actual results

💡 Pro Tips:

  • Always use real numbers — fake percentages hurt credibility
  • Add the client company logo if you have permission
  • Use case studies on your LinkedIn and website portfolio

5️⃣ QR Code Generator 📱

Purpose: Create QR codes for any website link, text, phone number, or email address.

How to use step by step:

  1. Click "QR Generator" on home page
  2. In the input field, type or paste:
    • A website URL: https://yourwebsite.com
    • A phone number: +923001234567
    • An email: yourname@gmail.com
    • Any text you want
  3. Click "Generate QR Code"
  4. Your QR code will appear below
  5. Right-click on the QR code image
  6. Select "Save image as"
  7. Save to your computer and use anywhere

💡 Pro Tips:

  • Use QR codes on business cards, flyers, and brochures
  • Always test the QR code with your phone before printing
  • Short URLs work better in QR codes than long ones

6️⃣ Image Compressor 🖼️

Purpose: Reduce image file size for faster websites and easier sharing without losing visible quality.

How to use step by step:

  1. Click "Image Compressor" on home page
  2. Click "Choose File" or drag your image
  3. Select any JPG, PNG or image file from your computer
  4. Click "Compress Image"
  5. You will see Original and Compressed images side by side
  6. Compare the file sizes shown below each image
  7. Right-click on the Compressed image
  8. Select "Save image as" to download

💡 Pro Tips:

  • Best for website images — smaller size means faster loading
  • Use before uploading to WhatsApp, Facebook or email
  • Works best with JPG photos — PNG files compress less

7️⃣ Meeting Minutes Generator 📋

Purpose: Convert rough meeting notes into a professional formatted summary to share with your team.

How to use step by step:

  1. Click "Meeting Minutes Gen" on home page
  2. During your meeting, write quick notes — one point per line
  3. After meeting, paste all notes in the text area
  4. Click "Generate Minutes"
  5. Review the formatted summary
  6. Copy the result
  7. Paste in email and add:
    • Names of all attendees
    • Meeting duration
    • Action item owners and deadlines
  8. Send to all meeting participants

💡 Pro Tips:

  • Send meeting minutes within 24 hours while memory is fresh
  • Always include clear action items with deadlines
  • Keep one line per discussion point for best formatting

8️⃣ Google Review Reply Generator ⭐

Purpose: Generate professional responses to customer Google reviews — both positive and negative.

How to use step by step:

  1. Go to your Google Business Profile
  2. Find the review you want to reply to
  3. Copy the full review text
  4. Come back and click "Google Review Reply"
  5. Paste the review in the text area
  6. Click "Generate Reply"
  7. The tool automatically detects positive or negative tone
  8. Copy the reply
  9. Personalize with customer name and specific details
  10. Paste and post on Google Business Profile

💡 Pro Tips:

  • Always reply to ALL reviews — positive and negative
  • Reply within 24-48 hours for best impression
  • Never argue with negative reviews — stay professional
  • For negative reviews, always offer to resolve privately

9️⃣ PC Configuration Advisor 🖥️

Purpose: Get the best PC build recommendation based on your purpose and budget.

How to use step by step:

  1. Click "PC Config Advisor" on home page
  2. Select your purpose from dropdown:
    • Gaming — For games and streaming
    • Video Editing — For content creators
    • Programming — For developers
    • Office Work — For daily business tasks
    • Graphic Design — For designers
  3. Move the budget slider to your amount ($500 to $5000)
  4. Click "Get Recommendation"
  5. Review the CPU, GPU, RAM and Storage recommendations
  6. Note down the parts and search prices on local market

💡 Pro Tips:

  • Always add 10-15% to budget for cables, OS and accessories
  • Check local prices — USD prices shown may differ in your country
  • For Pakistan: multiply USD price by current exchange rate
  • Buy from trusted shops with warranty for all components
